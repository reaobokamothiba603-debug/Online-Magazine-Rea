Technology in Lesotho

By: Reaoboka Mothiba Maseru

Technology is changing life in Lesotho in many positive ways. Communication has become faster through apps like WhatsApp and Facebook, helping people stay connected easily. In education, students use the internet and platforms like Google Classroom to access learning materials. Businesses, especially in Maseru, use social media and services like EcoCash to advertise and handle money quickly. Healthcare is also improving through digital systems that help doctors manage patient information better. However, challenges like poor internet access and high data costs still affect many people. Overall, technology is making life easier and more connected in Lesotho.

Cultural Festival 2026

By:Reaoboka Mothiba Maseru

The Cultural Festival 2026 in Lesotho celebrates the rich traditions of the Basotho people and brings communities together. It is a time to showcase culture through music, dance, food, and storytelling. Performances like mokhibo and famo, along with traditional attire such as the Basotho blanket, highlight cultural pride and identity. The festival, often held in Maseru, also attracts visitors and supports local businesses. Overall, the event helps preserve culture and reminds young people of the importance of their heritage.
